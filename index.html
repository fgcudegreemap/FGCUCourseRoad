FGCU Degree Mapper

This tool is not endorsed by Florida Gulf Coast University and is only intended to supplement the advising process. Please feel free to map out your degree requirements, see how to satisfy your prerequisite(s), and get information about classes.

To use this tool, select your major(s) and minor(s) and add classes by course number to the appropriate semester in your course schedule. If you want to "hide" long lists of extra classes that fulfill a particular requirement after you've completed it, select that option as well. Click on the classes in your schedule to see what requirements and prerequisite(s) you have met or still require. Please note, the system currently only accepts courses listed in the FGCU Course Catalog for the Fall 2016 semester. Enter courses in the format "ABC XXXX" or "ABC XXXXX" without quotations.

Please note, this current version of the Degree Mapper does not support corequisite courses. You can enter them, but there's still some work to be done to register that your class requires a corequisite. Also, if you have AP, transfer, or other non-coursework credits, please enter them as an equivalent FGCU class, we aren't able to support those kinds of inputs yet. Beyond that, with questions, errors, missing courses, etcetera, please email Jackson Graves at jrgraves@mit.edu with feedback to get it fixed.
